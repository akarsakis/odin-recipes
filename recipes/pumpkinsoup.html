Pumpkin Soup

Description

Keep warm with a bowl of soup

Ingredients

  • 1 whole butternut Pumpkin
  • 2 sprigs of spring onion
  • 2 potatoes
  • spices
  • Veg stock

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Cut pumpkin into wedges and add to oven
  3. Dice spring onion
  4. add to pot and cook with oil
  5. add spices and stock
  6. add potatos to pot and boil
  7. add pumpkin to pot and blend to desired consistency
