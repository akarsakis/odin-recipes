Healthy Breakfast Smoothie

Description

A healthy breakfast smoothie packed full of nutrients that will keep you charged through the morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • 1/2 cup of oats
  • 1/2 cup of nut milk
  • 1 tbl spoon cacao nibs
  • 1/2 tbl spoon shredded coconut

Steps

  1. chop bananas into small slices for easy blending
  2. place chopped bananas into blender
  3. add cacao nibs and shredded coconut to blender
  4. add nut milk and blend ingredients until desired consistency
